What axolotls are

Axolotls are salamanders. Unlike most salamanders they stay in their adolescent stage where they have external gills but also have lungs. They also have the amazing ability to regenerate any body part (including the brain, heart and everything else)
that may get damaged or removed. In the wild they live in Lake Xochimilco, Mexico city, Mexico

Where you can find axolotls in everyday life.

You can find axolotls everywere; as a plush in your local store or even on the Mexican $50 bill.

Axolotls for pets

Axolotls make great pets. As a baby they cost from $20-$40 and as an adult they cost around $90-$150. They are easy to take care of and if you forget to feed once or twice its fine. You only have to clean their tank once or twice a month. Those are reasons you should get an axolotl over a fish or a lizard.

You can buy axolotls from Axolotl Planet or Ivys Axolotls, or maybe some people will have some in your neighborhood who breed them to sell.
